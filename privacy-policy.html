Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MegaMocks is a collection of free browser-based study tools. We take your privacy seriously. This page explains exactly what we collect, how we use it, and what we don't do.

The Short Version

✅ All tools run entirely in your browser
✅ No study data you enter is sent to any server
✅ We do not require any account or login
✅ We do not collect your name, email, or personal details (unless you contact us)
⚠️ We use third-party advertising (Google AdSense) which may use cookies
⚠️ We use basic analytics to understand site traffic (no personal identification)

What We Collect

Tool Inputs

When you use any tool on MegaMocks, the data you enter (subject names, hours, dates, ratings, etc.) is processed entirely within your own browser. None of this data is sent to our servers or stored anywhere by us. Some tools optionally save your inputs to your browser's local storage — this stays on your device only.

Browser Local Storage

Some tools offer a "Save" feature. This uses your browser's local storage — a storage mechanism built into every browser that keeps data on your device only. We cannot access it. You can clear it anytime by clearing your browser's data for this site.

Analytics

We may use anonymised website analytics to understand how many people visit the site, which pages are popular, and how users navigate between tools. This data is aggregated — it does not identify you personally.

Contact Form

If you use our contact form, you provide a name, email address, and message. We use this only to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your contact details with third parties.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

MegaMocks uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your interests and previous visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's ad settings.

We do not control which ads are shown and have no access to any data collected by Google through their advertising service.

Cookies

MegaMocks itself uses minimal cookies — primarily for your light/dark mode preference. Third-party services (Google AdSense, analytics) may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

MegaMocks is suitable for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No tool requires any personal data to function. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us.

Data Security

Because tool data never leaves your device, there is very little data security risk associated with using MegaMocks tools. The contact form uses HTTPS encryption for any data transmitted.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (contact form submissions only)
  • Request deletion of any contact data we hold
  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove any saved tool data
  • Opt out of Google's personalised advertising

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new date. Continued use of MegaMocks after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Use our contact page to reach us.
