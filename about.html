About MegaMocks

Why we built this, who it's for, and what we believe about studying.

MegaMocks started from a simple frustration: there were hundreds of complicated study apps, premium planners, and subscription-based tools — but nothing simple, free, and actually useful for a student sitting at their desk at 11 PM trying to figure out if they were actually ready for their exam tomorrow.

We wanted something different. Small tools. Honest results. No account. No subscription. No emails asking you to upgrade.

What MegaMocks Is

MegaMocks is a collection of free, browser-based study tools for students. Each tool solves one specific problem — like finding out which topics you haven't revised in too long, checking if your daily study plan is actually feasible, or figuring out the best order to attempt exam questions.

Every tool works on your device, right in your browser. No data is ever sent to a server. No account is needed. No login, no tracking, no selling your information.

Who It's For

MegaMocks is for any student preparing for any exam — school board exams, university finals, competitive entrance tests, professional certifications. If you're studying for something and you want honest, practical tools to help you prepare better, this is for you.

We pay particular attention to students who study on mobile phones — which is most of us. Every page and tool is designed to work beautifully on a small screen.

What We Believe About Studying

Studying hard is not the same as studying smart. Time matters less than how you use it.

Honest self-assessment is a skill. Most students overestimate what they know until a practice test shows them otherwise.

A consistent, realistic plan beats an ambitious one you abandon on Day 3.

We built MegaMocks around these beliefs. Every tool asks you to be honest about your actual situation — not your ideal one. The results are only as useful as the inputs are truthful.

How We Keep It Free

MegaMocks is supported by display advertising. We keep ads non-intrusive, clearly separated from tool content, and we never let advertisements affect what the tools tell you. The tools give honest results regardless of anything else.

We will never charge for access to any tool on this site. That's a commitment we take seriously.

A Note on Accuracy

MegaMocks tools are decision-support tools, not guarantees. A good exam readiness score doesn't guarantee you'll pass. A bad one doesn't mean you'll fail. The tools are mirrors — they reflect your inputs back to you in a more structured way, and they suggest actions based on common patterns. What you do with that is entirely up to you.

The most important thing is that you use them honestly. The tools can't help you if you rate yourself more optimistically than you actually are.
