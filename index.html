Study Tips That Actually Make Sense

Not generic advice. Real stuff that changes how you study.

Studying for 8 hours means nothing if you forget everything after 2 days. Revise shorter, revise more often.

The feeling of being ready and actually being ready are two different things. Test yourself before you walk in.

Your phone is not a 5-minute break. It is a 45-minute black hole. Put it in another room.

Reading your notes is not revision. Closing your notes and trying to recall from memory — that is revision.

The night before an exam is not the time to learn new things. Rest is productive too.

One difficult topic per day beats ten easy topics you already know. Fix weaknesses before they cost you marks.
