How This Tool Works

For each topic, you rate how complete your notes feel from 1 (barely started) to 5 (detailed and complete). The tool calculates an average completeness score and flags any topic rated 2 or below as a priority gap.

Study Tips

  • → Incomplete notes are not a failure — they're just information. Now you know what to fix.
  • → Borrow notes from a classmate for gaps — don't relearn from scratch if you don't have time.
  • → Notes don't need to be perfect. They need to be useful for revision.
