How This Tool Works

The tool calculates the percentage of your total study time going to each subject. If any subject is getting significantly more or less than a balanced share, it flags it. The ideal share depends on your goal — equal spread, or weighted by difficulty.

Why Load Balance Matters

It's natural to spend more time on subjects you enjoy or feel comfortable in. But exams test everything. The subject you keep putting off is usually the one that costs you the most marks.

Study Tips

  • → The subjects you avoid are usually the ones that need the most time. Face them first.
  • → Set a minimum floor for each subject — for example, no subject gets less than 2 hours per week.
  • → Update this tool at the end of each week to make sure you're not drifting.
